Instruments used in the Classical Era

The Piano

The Piano is a percussion instrument. Still used today in Modern Electronic Music,
it is one of the most famous instruments in the music industries.
The Piano was founded by Bartolomeo Cristofori
near the year 1700 in modern day Italy.
One of the main piano manufacturers are Steinway, founded in New York by
German Immigrant Henry E. Steinway,and Yamaha, founded by
Japanese businessman Torakusu Yamaha. Some famous pianists are Frederic Chopin , Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart and Ludwig van Beethoven

The Violin
The Violin is a String Instrument, meaning you have to draw a bow to play it. One of the most popular and learnt instruments in the world,
is founded by Andrea Amati, an Italian String Instrument Maker.
Amati founded the Violin near the year 1550. Some famous violinists are Antonio Vivaldi , Niccolo Paganini and Johann Sebastian
