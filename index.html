The Classical Music Era was in the 18th Century. Many famous composers lived during this era.
The Classical Music we know today, mostly originated from Austria, Germany and France.
On this website, you will read about the greatest Classical Composers and their life's great work.



Instruments used in the Classical Era

The Piano

The Piano is a percussion instrument. Still used today in Modern Electronic Music,
it is one of the most famous instruments in the music industries.
The Piano was invented by Bartolomeo Cristofori
near the year 1700 in modern day Italy.
Some famous pianists are Frederic Chopin,
Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart and Ludwig van Beethoven.

The Violin

The Violin is a String Instrument, meaning you have to draw a bow to play it.
One of the most popular and learnt instruments in the world, the Violin
is invented by Andrea Amati, an Italian String Instrument Maker.
Amati invented the Violin near the year 1550. Some famous violinists
are Antonio Vivaldi, Niccolo Paganini and
Johann Sebastian Bach.
